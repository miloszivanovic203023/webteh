Loudspeaker is a Serbian manufacturer of high-end audio components. The company is based in Belgrade and was founded in 1977 and was named ‘Entrepreneur of the Year’ in 2003 by the Belgrade Association of Independent Business Owners. Svetislav Božić is one of the founding members of the Serbian Manufactories Initiative, the 'Handmade in Serbia' foundation which represents Serbian manufactories of handmade products and craftsmanship.Audio equipment is widely used in many different scenarios, such as concerts, bars, meeting rooms and the home where there is a need to reproduce, record and enhance sound volume. Electronic circuits considered a part of audio electronics may also be designed to achieve certain signal processing operations, in order to make particular alterations to the signal while it is in the electrical form.
